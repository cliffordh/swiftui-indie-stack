# Terms of Service

This is a placeholder Terms of Service page.

## For Template Users

Replace this URL in `AppConfiguration.swift` with your actual Terms of Service before submitting to the App Store.

```swift
static let termsOfServiceURL = "https://yourapp.com/terms"
```

## Requirements

Apple requires all apps to have accessible Terms of Service and Privacy Policy links. You can host these on:

- Your app's website
- A GitHub Pages site
- Notion (with public sharing)
- Any publicly accessible URL
